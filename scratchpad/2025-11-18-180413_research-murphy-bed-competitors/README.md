## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **King-Size Availability:** Some brands limit king-size offerings; always verify availability before ordering
2. **Pricing Verification:** Contact vendors directly for current pricing; listed prices may change
3. **Warranty:** Check warranty terms carefully; DIY kits typically have lifetime hardware warranty but vary on labor/installation
4. **Lead Times:** 
   - DIY kits: 3-5 days
   - Complete units: 4-8 weeks
   - Alibaba imports: 8-14 weeks
5. **Shipping:** Factor in $100-400 for delivery on most units
6. **Installation:** DIY kits require self-assembly; other options vary
7. **Quality:** Price correlates with material quality; lower-cost imports may require quality verification
