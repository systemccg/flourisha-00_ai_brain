## KEY DECISION FACTORS

### Choose DIY Hardware ($349-400) If:
- You have woodworking skills (or willing to learn)
- You have 5-7 days free
- You have tools or access to tool rental
- Budget is absolute priority

### Choose Mechanism Kit ($1,047) If:
- You want quality with warranty
- You have budget for custom cabinet ($800-2,000)
- You don't want to build cabinet yourself
- You want 10-30% savings vs Wilding

### Choose Cabinet Bed ($1,200-2,000) If:
- You're renting (no wall mounting)
- You want portability
- You need minimal installation
- Convenience matters as much as cost

### Choose Wall Bed King ($TBD, 40% off) If:
