KEY FINDINGS
============

SEVERANCE COMPLIANCE:
- Employees earning <10x min wage: 30 days (year 1) + 20 days (subsequent years)
- Employees earning ≥10x min wage: 20 days (year 1) + 15 days (subsequent years)
- Plus: 1 month salary annual cesantías contribution + 12% interest
- Deel's offboarding calculator automates these calculations

VACATION COMPLIANCE:
- 15 working days minimum annually
- Accrues at 1⅔ days/month
- Can be monetized up to 50%
- Must be paid upon termination if unused
- Deel tracks accrual and calculates termination payouts

SOCIAL SECURITY COMPLIANCE:
- Four required components: EPS, AFP, ARL, CCF
- Employer contributions: 21.02%-27.46% of salary
- PILA monthly reporting required
- Deel automates registration, calculation, and PILA submission
- Non-compliance penalties: up to 5,000 UVTs (~$55,000)
