# Research Query Decomposition: Deel.com for 501c3 Organizations

## Original Question
Can Deel.com help a 501c3 nonprofit organization manage all benefits for 2 employees in the USA and 2 employees in Colombia?

## 24 Research Sub-Questions (Extensive Mode)

### Deel Platform Capabilities (8 questions)
1. What are Deel's core services and how does their global HR platform work for international teams?
2. Does Deel specifically support 501c3 nonprofit organizations and are there any special pricing or features for nonprofits?
3. What employee benefits does Deel offer for US-based employees (health insurance, retirement, PTO, etc.)?
4. What employee benefits does Deel provide for Colombia-based employees and how do they comply with Colombian labor laws?
5. Can Deel handle both US employees and international contractors/employees on the same platform?
6. What is Deel's pricing structure and what are the total costs for managing 4 employees (2 US, 2 Colombia)?
7. How does Deel handle payroll processing, tax withholding, and compliance for US employees?
8. What are Deel's limitations or restrictions for small organizations (4 employees)?

### Colombia-Specific Capabilities (8 questions)
9. Does Deel have an Employer of Record (EOR) service in Colombia and how does it work?
10. What are the mandatory employee benefits in Colombia and can Deel manage all of them?
11. How does Deel handle Colombian labor law compliance including severance, vacation, and social security?
12. What is the cost difference between hiring employees directly in Colombia vs using Deel's EOR service?
13. Can Deel manage Colombian health insurance (EPS), pension (AFP), and other mandatory benefits?
14. How does Deel handle Colombian payroll taxes, income tax withholding, and year-end tax forms?
15. What are the legal requirements for a US 501c3 to employ workers in Colombia?
16. Does Deel provide local HR support and expertise for Colombian employment issues?

### US Employee Benefits & Compliance (8 questions)
17. What health insurance options does Deel offer for US employees and how do they compare to traditional plans?
18. Does Deel offer 401(k) or other retirement benefits for US employees at nonprofits?
19. How does Deel handle US payroll taxes, state taxes, and compliance across different states?
20. Can Deel manage workers' compensation insurance, unemployment insurance, and other mandatory US benefits?
21. What PTO, sick leave, and other leave management features does Deel provide for US employees?
22. How does Deel compare to traditional PEO (Professional Employer Organization) services for US employees?
23. Are there any IRS or Department of Labor restrictions on 501c3 organizations using Deel's services?
24. What are alternative platforms to Deel for managing international + US employees for nonprofits (Gusto, Rippling, Remote.com, etc.)?

## Research Strategy
- Launch 8 perplexity-researcher agents for questions 1-8
- Launch 8 claude-researcher agents for questions 9-16
- Launch 8 gemini-researcher agents for questions 17-24
- 10-minute timeout for extensive research mode
- Synthesize findings with confidence levels and source attribution
