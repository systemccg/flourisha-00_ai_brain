## Executive Summary

Deel offers both a traditional **US PEO model** ($79-$95/employee/month) and a **lightweight US Payroll alternative** ($19/employee/month), positioning itself as a more modern, globally-integrated alternative to legacy PEO services. Traditional PEOs like ADP TotalSource, Paychex, and Insperity charge $100-$250/employee/month and focus primarily on domestic operations.

The key distinction: **Traditional PEOs are co-employers with shared legal liability; Deel's Payroll service is not, though Deel's PEO option does provide co-employment.**

## Part 1: Understanding the Models

### Traditional PEO Model

A **Professional Employer Organization (PEO)** is a full-service HR outsourcing solution based on **co-employment**:

#### How Traditional PEOs Work
- **Co-Employment Relationship:** The PEO becomes the statutory employer for tax purposes and shares employer responsibilities with the client company
- **Employer of Record:** The PEO files payroll taxes under its own tax identification number (EIN)
- **Shared Liability:** Legal and compliance responsibilities are shared between the PEO and the client company
- **Full-Service HR:** The PEO handles all HR administration, payroll, benefits, compliance, and risk management

#### Traditional PEO Services Included
1. **Payroll Administration**
   - Paycheck processing
   - Tax withholding and calculations
   - Wage garnishment handling

2. **Benefits Administration**
   - Health insurance negotiation
   - Workers' compensation insurance
   - General liability insurance
   - Employment practices liability insurance
   - Retirement plans (401k, etc.)

3. **Tax Compliance & Filing**
   - Federal, state, and local payroll taxes
   - Unemployment insurance taxes
   - Workers' compensation coverage
   - Automatic tax filing with PEO's EIN

4. **HR Management & Compliance**
   - Employee handbook development
   - Policy creation and updates
   - Employee classification guidance
   - Compliance with federal and state labor laws
   - HR consulting support

5. **Risk Management**
   - Employment practices liability mitigation
   - Compliance oversight across all 50 states
   - Legal protection through shared liability

### Deel's Model: Dual Approach

Deel uniquely offers **two distinct options** for US employees:

#### Option 1: Deel US Payroll (Payroll Service Provider)
- **NOT co-employment**: Deel is a payroll processor, not an employer
- **What Deel handles:** Payroll processing, tax calculations, contractor management
- **What the client handles:** Legal liability, HR management, compliance responsibility
- **Similar to:** Traditional payroll service providers (not true PEOs)
- **Price:** $19-$59/employee/month

#### Option 2: Deel US PEO (Co-Employment Model)
- **IS co-employment**: Deel becomes a statutory employer
- **What Deel handles:** Full HR, payroll, benefits, compliance, tax filing
- **What the client handles:** Day-to-day management and organizational decisions
- **Similar to:** Traditional PEOs
- **Price:** $79-$95/employee/month

## Part 5: Legal and Liability Comparison

### Co-Employment and Liability Frameworks

#### PEO Co-Employment Model (Deel PEO & Traditional PEOs)
**Shared Responsibility Structure:**
- PEO is the **statutory employer** for tax and legal purposes
- Files payroll taxes under PEO's EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- **Shares legal liability** for employment practices
- **Shares compliance responsibility** for labor laws

**Legal Protections:**
- PEO shares liability for employment lawsuits
- Tax compliance errors are shared responsibility
- Compliance violations may be shared liability
- Workers' compensation claims handled by PEO
- Less legal exposure for the business owner

#### Payroll Service Provider Model (Deel Payroll)
**Clear Responsibility Structure:**
- Client company remains the **employer of record**
- All taxes filed under client's EIN
- **Client bears all legal liability** for compliance
- **Client responsible** for employment law compliance

**Legal Exposures:**
- All employment lawsuits are client's responsibility
- Tax compliance errors are client's liability
- Workers' compensation claims are client's responsibility
- Compliance violations fall entirely on employer
- Client has full legal exposure

### Tax and Compliance Responsibility

| Aspect | Deel Payroll | Deel PEO | Traditional PEO |
|--------|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| **Tax Filing** | Client EIN | PEO's EIN | PEO's EIN |
| **Liability for Tax Errors** | Client | Shared | Shared |
| **Employment Law Compliance** | Client | Shared | Shared |
| **HR Policy Development** | Client | PEO | PEO |
| **Workers' Compensation** | Client | PEO | PEO |
| **Legal Liability** | Client | Shared | Shared |

## Conclusion

**Deel offers a compelling modern alternative to traditional PEO services for US employers**, particularly those prioritizing cost efficiency and platform simplicity. The company's dual offering provides flexibility:

- **Deel Payroll** serves budget-conscious companies with internal HR expertise
- **Deel PEO** captures mid-market companies seeking co-employment protection at significantly lower costs than traditional PEOs

**Traditional PEOs remain superior for:**
- Large enterprises (100+ employees) benefiting from negotiated rates at scale
- Industry-specific compliance needs
- Organizations requiring specialized risk management
- Companies with legacy system integration requirements

The choice ultimately depends on company size, existing HR capabilities, budget constraints, and whether international expansion is planned.
