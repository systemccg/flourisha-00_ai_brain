## Executive Summary

Deel is an all-in-one Global People Platform founded in 2019 that simplifies hiring, paying, and managing international teams across 150+ countries. The platform integrates global payroll, HR management (HRIS), compliance, benefits administration, and talent management into a single unified system. Deel serves over 25,000+ companies worldwide, from startups to publicly traded enterprises.
