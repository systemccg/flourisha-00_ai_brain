## Research Question

**Does Deel offer 401(k) or other retirement benefits for US employees at nonprofits?**

### Focus Areas
- 401(k) availability and features
- Nonprofit-specific retirement options (403b)
- Employer match capabilities
- Administration and fees
- Alternative retirement benefits

---

## Key Findings Summary

### 401(k) Availability: YES
Deel offers 401(k) retirement plans through three service models:
1. **EOR (Employer of Record):** Fidelity-administered with automatic enrollment
2. **PEO (Professional Employer Organization):** Flexible setup with comprehensive benefits
3. **Direct US Payroll:** Integrated retirement plan management through Deel platform

### 403(b) for Nonprofits: NOT DOCUMENTED
No evidence found in public sources that Deel currently offers 403(b) tax-sheltered annuity plans specifically for 501(c)(3) organizations. Recommendation: Contact Deel directly for confirmation.

### Employer Match: 2% Mandatory (EOR)
- EOR clients required to provide 2% match per pay period
- Match rate **cannot be increased or decreased** (fixed)
- Fidelity serves as plan custodian
- PEO clients have more flexibility (contact directly)

### Plan Administration: Comprehensive
- Integrated in Deel Payroll with direct employee enrollment
- Automatic data syncing with Fidelity
- 100+ third-party integrations available
- All compliance and tax filing handled by Deel

### Fees: Transparent
- No hidden fees; pricing included in quoted rates
- Month-to-month flexible billing
- No setup, transfer, or tax filing prep fees documented

### Alternative Retirement Benefits: Available
- Non-Qualified Deferred Compensation (NQDC)
- Individual Retirement Accounts (IRAs, SEP IRAs, Roth IRAs)
- Solo 401(k)
- Health Savings Accounts (HSAs)
- Roth 401(k)
- Partnership with Betterment at Work for financial planning
