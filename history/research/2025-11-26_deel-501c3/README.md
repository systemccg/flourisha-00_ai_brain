# Research: Deel.com for 501c3 Organizations

**Date:** 2025-11-26
**Research Mode:** Extensive (24 parallel agents)
**Agents Used:** 8 perplexity, 8 claude, 8 gemini

## Research Question
Can Deel.com help a 501c3 nonprofit organization manage all benefits for 2 employees in the USA and 2 employees in Colombia?

## Key Findings

### Overall Verdict: YES with qualifications

**Strengths:**
- ✅ All mandatory benefits managed in both countries
- ✅ Social Good program: First 2 employees FREE (Year 1)
- ✅ No Colombian legal entity required (EOR model)
- ✅ 5-day implementation timeline
- ✅ Comprehensive Colombian labor law compliance
- ✅ 24/7 Spanish-language support

**Limitations:**
- ⚠️ No documented 403(b) retirement plans (401k only)
- ⚠️ No ongoing nonprofit discounts after Year 1
- ⚠️ 4-state workers' comp gap (OH, ND, WA, WY)
- ⚠️ Higher cost than US-only alternatives

### Cost Analysis

**Year 1 (with Social Good):** $1,896-2,280
- 2 Colombia employees via EOR: FREE
- 2 US employees via PEO: $79-95/month each

**Year 2+ Options:**
- Full EOR/PEO: $16,512/year (simplest, most expensive)
- Global Payroll with entity: $922/year (cheapest, requires entity)
