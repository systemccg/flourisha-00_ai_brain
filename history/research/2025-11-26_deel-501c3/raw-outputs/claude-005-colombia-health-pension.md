## Executive Summary

Deel is a global payroll and HR compliance platform that **does manage Colombian mandatory benefits**, including health insurance (EPS), pension contributions (AFP), occupational hazards insurance (ARL), and family compensation fund contributions (Caja de Compensación). Deel handles these through its Employer of Record (EOR) services and operates in compliance with Colombia's PILA (Planilla Integrada de Liquidación de Aportes) system.

**Key Finding:** Deel calculates, withholds, and remits all required Colombian payroll taxes and mandatory social contributions monthly through the PILA system, with average onboarding time of 5 days.

## 1. Colombian Mandatory Benefits System Overview

### 1.1 Required Contributions Structure

Colombian employment law mandates comprehensive social security contributions divided between employer and employee costs:

| Benefit | Type | Employee % | Employer % | Total % | Notes |
|---------|------|-----------|-----------|---------|-------|
| **Health Insurance (EPS)** | Health | 4% | 8.5% | 12.5% | Mandatory for all residents |
| **Pension Fund (AFP)** | Pension | 4% | 12% | 16% | From July 2025, new 4-pillar system |
| **Family Compensation (CCF)** | Parafiscal | 0% | 4% | 4% | Employer only |
| **SENA** | Training | 0% | 2% | 2% | Employer only (National Training Service) |
| **ICBF** | Social | 0% | 3% | 3% | Employer only (Family Welfare Institute) |
| **ARL** | Occupational | 0% | 0.522%-6.96% | Variable | Employer only; varies by job risk level |

**Total Employer Cost Impact:** Approximately 40-50% of base salary in total contributions

## 2. EPS (Salud - Health Insurance) Management

### 2.1 What is EPS?

EPS stands for **Empresas Prestadoras de Salud** (Health Service Providers). All Colombian residents must be registered with an EPS or subsidized health insurance provider.

### 2.2 Deel's EPS Management Capabilities

**Deel Responsibilities:**
- Mandatory registration of employees with an EPS provider
- Monthly withholding of 4% employee contribution from salary
- Monthly remittance of 8.5% employer contribution
- Management of EPS enrollment documentation
- Compliance with employee healthcare service provisions

**Key Features:**
- Deel manages required health insurance registration and enrollment
- Automatic calculation and withholding of EPS contributions
- Integration with PILA system for monthly submission
- Provides ongoing consultation on health insurance compliance requirements

### 2.3 Enrollment Requirements

- Employees must provide Colombian identification (Cédula)
- EPS enrollment begins immediately upon application approval
- Coverage includes compulsory health plan with preventive and treatment services
- Individual EPS choice available within approved providers

### 2.4 Limitations

**Important Restriction:** Under Resolution 5477 of 2022, Business Visas (V) and Retirement Visas (M) holders **cannot affiliate with EPS**. These visa holders cannot utilize the Colombian social security system.

**Source:** Deel manages EPS enrollment but must verify visa status eligibility before registration.

## 3. AFP (Pension Fund Contributions) Management

### 3.1 What is AFP?

AFP stands for **Administradora de Fondos de Pensiones** (Pension Fund Administrator). Pension contributions are mandatory for all employed individuals in Colombia.

### 3.2 Recent Pension System Reform (July 2025)

Effective July 1, 2025, Colombia implemented Law 2281 introducing a new 4-pillar pension system:

1. **Solidarity Pillar** - Minimum income guarantee
2. **Semi-Contributory Pillar** - For lower-income workers
3. **Contributory Pillar** - Standard employee/employer contributions
4. **Voluntary Savings Pillar** - Optional additional savings

**Mixed System Requirement:** All individuals contributing to pensions must have:
- Minimum contribution in public pension fund (Colpensiones)
- Excess contributions (over threshold) directed to private AFP if income exceeds certain amounts

### 3.3 Deel's AFP Management Capabilities

**Deel Responsibilities:**
- Mandatory pension fund registration and enrollment
- Monthly calculation of 4% employee contribution (withheld from salary)
- Monthly calculation of 12% employer contribution
- Routing of contributions through PILA system
- Compliance with new 4-pillar system requirements as of July 2025

**Key Features:**
- Automatic calculation of pension contributions
- Selection and management of appropriate AFP provider
- Withholding and remittance of both employee and employer portions
- Compliance with mixed public-private pension requirement
- Monthly PILA submission ensuring legal compliance

### 3.4 Contribution Structure

- **Employee Contribution:** 4% of gross salary (mandatory, withheld)
- **Employer Contribution:** 12% of gross salary
- **Total Pension Cost:** 16% of employee compensation

## 4. ARL (Occupational Hazards Insurance) Management

### 4.1 What is ARL?

ARL stands for **Administradora de Riesgos Laborales** (Occupational Risk Administrator). Colombian labor law mandates that employers must enroll workers with an ARL insurer.

### 4.2 ARL Coverage and Functions

ARL provides:
- **Prevention:** Workplace safety and hazard prevention programs
- **Protection:** Risk mitigation measures for workers
- **Care:** Medical attention for work-related injuries and illnesses
- **Benefits:**
  - Health service expenses for occupational risk care
  - Work accident and occupational disease benefits
  - Incapacity payments
  - Disability pensions
  - Survivor pensions

### 4.3 ARL Contribution Rates

**Rate Structure:** Variable based on occupational risk classification (0.522% to 6.96% of monthly salary)

**Examples:**
- Low-risk office work: ~0.5-1.0%
- Medium-risk roles: ~2-3%
- High-risk positions: ~5-7%

**Paid By:** Employer only (100% employer cost)

### 4.4 Deel's ARL Management Capabilities

**Deel Responsibilities:**
- Mandatory ARL registration with appropriate provider
- Correct risk classification of employee positions
- Monthly calculation of ARL contributions based on job classification
- Automatic remittance of ARL premiums through PILA
- Compliance with occupational safety regulations
- Workplace safety guidance and consultation

**Key Features:**
- Proper job classification for accurate rate determination
- Monthly ARL premium calculation and submission
- Integration with Colombia's social security registration system
- Ongoing consultation on workplace safety regulations and ARL compliance

### 4.5 Major ARL Providers

- ARL SURA (largest provider)
- Positive ARL
- Libertad Colombia
- Additional regional providers

## 5. Caja de Compensación (Family Compensation Fund) Management

### 5.1 What is Caja de Compensación?

**Caja de Compensación Familiar** (Family Compensation Funds) are non-profit legal entities in Colombia that:
- Administer family subsidy programs
- Subject to state control and supervision
- Provide social services to workers and their families
- Improve quality of life through family benefits

### 5.2 Coverage and Services

Cajas de Compensación provide:
- Family subsidies
- Recreation and leisure services
- Education and training programs
- Healthcare and wellness benefits
- Housing assistance programs
- Childcare support

### 5.3 Contribution and Jurisdiction

**Contribution Rate:** 4% of employee base salary (Employer only)

**Coverage:** 43 Cajas nationwide with departmental jurisdiction serving 22+ million Colombians (workers and beneficiaries)

**Selection:** Employees typically choose from available Cajas in their department

### 5.4 Deel's Caja Management Capabilities

**Deel Responsibilities:**
- Mandatory registration of employees with a Caja de Compensación
- Monthly calculation of 4% employer contribution
- Remittance of contributions through PILA system
- Management of Caja enrollment and selection documentation
- Compliance with Caja regulations and service provisions

**Key Features:**
- Automatic enrollment in available Caja options
- Monthly contribution calculation (4% of base salary)
- PILA integration for systematic contribution remittance
- Support for employee benefit access and services

### 5.5 The 43 Cajas in Colombia

Each Caja operates with departmental jurisdiction. Examples include:
- CAFAM (largest and most well-known)
- Compensar
- Comcaja (for rural/agricultural workers)
- Regional and sector-specific Cajas

## 6. SENA and ICBF Contributions Management

### 6.1 SENA (Servicio Nacional de Aprendizaje)

**Purpose:** National Training Service funding vocational training and apprenticeships

**Contribution:** 2% of monthly payroll (Employer only)

**Deel's Role:** Automatic calculation and monthly remittance through PILA

### 6.2 ICBF (Instituto Colombiano de Bienestar Familiar)

**Purpose:** Colombian Family Welfare Institute - supports family and child development programs

**Contribution:** 3% of monthly payroll (Employer only)

**Deel's Role:** Automatic calculation and monthly remittance through PILA

### 6.3 Parafiscal Contribution Caps

For employees earning above 10x the minimum wage:
- Employer must contribute 9% parafiscal (not 9% each)
- Distributed as: SENA (2%) + ICBF (3%) + CCF (4%)

## 7. PILA System Integration

### 7.1 What is PILA?

**PILA** = **Planilla Integrada de Liquidación de Aportes** (Integrated Contribution Settlement Worksheet)

Colombia's mandatory centralized system for monthly processing and submission of all payroll contributions.

### 7.2 What PILA Covers

Single monthly submission consolidating:
- Health insurance (EPS) contributions
- Pension fund (AFP) contributions
- Occupational risk (ARL) contributions
- Family compensation (CCF) contributions
- SENA contributions
- ICBF contributions
- Income tax withholding
- All statutory deductions

### 7.3 PILA Submission Requirements

**Timing:** Monthly submissions with deadlines based on employer tax ID (NIT)

**Penalties:** Late or incorrect submissions result in significant penalties

**Verification:** PILA cross-references with DIAN (tax authority) and social security entities

### 7.4 Deel's PILA Management

**Deel Responsibilities:**
- Calculate all contributions correctly
- Consolidate contributions into PILA format
- Submit PILA monthly within required deadlines
- Maintain compliance documentation
- Handle DIAN (tax authority) coordination

**Key Compliance Feature:** Deel manages the entire PILA process, eliminating manual calculation errors and ensuring timely submission.

## 9. Key Limitations and Considerations

### 9.1 Visa Restrictions

**EPS Enrollment Restrictions:**
- Business Visa (V) holders: Cannot use EPS
- Retirement Visa (M) holders: Cannot use EPS
- These visa holders cannot affiliate with Colombian social security

**Implication for Deel:** Deel cannot process EPS enrollment for employees on restricted visas

### 9.2 Foreigners and Social Security

**Cédula de Extranjería Required:**
Foreigners residing in Colombia with Cédula de Extranjería (Foreign ID) are legally required to contribute to Colombian social security, including:
- EPS (health)
- AFP (pension)
- ARL (occupational)
- All parafiscal contributions

**Deel's Role:** Ensures proper registration and contributions for all eligible workers

### 9.3 Recent System Changes (2025)

New pension system (Law 2281) effective July 2025:
- Mixed public-private pension requirement
- Four-pillar system implementation
- Deel actively implements these new requirements
