## Executive Summary

Deel provides a comprehensive leave management platform that handles PTO tracking, accrual, approvals, and payroll integration for US employers. The platform supports configurable leave policies, state-specific sick leave compliance, FMLA protections, and integration with payroll processing. Deel offers both limited accrual policies and unlimited PTO options, with features accessible through their web platform and Slack integration.

## 1. PTO Accrual and Tracking

### 1.1 Accrual Methods

Deel supports multiple PTO accrual calculation methods:

- **Hourly Accrual:** Calculated as Annual PTO Hours ÷ Total Work Hours (e.g., 120 hours ÷ 2,080 hours = 0.0577 hours per hour worked)
- **Pay Period Accrual:** Consistent accrual amounts per pay period (common for salaried employees)
- **Periodic Accrual:** One week, pay period, month, quarter, or annual accrual periods
- **Lump Sum Method:** Full annual PTO granted at the beginning of the year

### 1.2 Tracking Features

**Balance Visibility:**
- Employees can view their remaining vacation days balance through the Deel platform
- The system displays total vacation days entitled and days already used
- Employees can see the vacation accrual start date
- Real-time balance tracking available on employee homepages

**Time Off Requests:**
- Employees submit requests through "Request Time Off" button in the Time Off and Public Holidays section
- Requests include description fields for context
- Employees can track request status in real-time
- System shows estimated days being requested and impact on remaining balance

### 1.3 Accrual Accrual Calculations

Common accrual rates in Deel:
- Approximately 1.5 hours of PTO per week = 2 weeks (10 business days) annually
- Customizable accrual schedules based on company policy
- Support for hourly and salaried employee distinctions

### 1.4 PTO Payout Management

**Payout Processing:**
- Employees with positive PTO balance upon resignation may receive payout
- Payout calculated as accrued but unused hours at time of separation
- Some states require mandatory PTO payout; Deel helps navigate state-specific requirements
- PTO payout is treated as wages and subject to federal, state, and local taxes
- Time Off Overview shows potential payouts for unused days

- No federal mandate for paid sick leave in the United States
- Department of Labor grants individual states and employers autonomy

- Michigan
- Minnesota
- Nevada (mandatory paid leave for any reason)
- New Jersey
- New Mexico
- New York (56 hours for 100+ employees; 40 hours for 5-99 employees)
- Oregon
- Rhode Island

- Washington

## 3. FMLA and Other Protected Leave

### 3.1 FMLA Coverage

Deel provides support for FMLA (Family and Medical Leave Act) compliance:

**FMLA Overview:**
- Federal law providing eligible employees up to 12 weeks unpaid, job-protected leave per year
- Applies to private employers with 50+ employees, public agencies, elementary/secondary schools

**FMLA Eligibility Requirements:**
- Work for covered employer (50+ employees)
- 12-month employment tenure (non-consecutive)
- 1,250 hours worked in past 12 months
- Work location with 50+ employees within 75 miles

**Qualifying Reasons for FMLA Leave:**
- Birth of child and newborn care (within one year)
- Care for family member with serious health condition
- Employee's own serious health condition
- Military caregiver leave (up to 26 weeks for covered servicemember)
- Qualifying exigency leave (active military duty)

### 3.2 Job Protections Under FMLA

- Employer must maintain group health insurance during FMLA leave
- Coverage continued under same terms and conditions as active employment
- Reinstatement to same or equivalent position upon return
- Equivalent pay, benefits, and employment terms guaranteed

### 3.3 Deel's FMLA Compliance Services

- Deel PEO helps offload HR and compliance risks
- FMLA tracking and documentation within platform
- Expert consultation available through demos
- Integration with overall leave management system

### 3.4 Other Protected Leave Categories

Deel supports configuration for various protected leave types:
- Military service leave
- Jury duty and court appearances
- Voting leave
- Victim's rights leave
- Domestic violence/abuse leave (varies by state)

## 4. Leave Request Workflows

### 4.1 Employee Request Process

**Step-by-Step Workflow:**

1. **Request Initiation:**
   - Employee clicks "Request Time Off" button in Time Off and Public Holidays section
   - Appears on employee homepage

2. **Request Details:**
   - Select leave type (Vacation, Sick Leave, Other)
   - Choose date range
   - Add description/notes
   - System calculates impact on remaining balance

3. **Submission:**
   - Employee submits request
   - System automatically routes to assigned approver(s)
   - Notification sent to manager

### 4.2 Manager/Approver Workflow

**Review Process:**

1. **Notification:**
   - Pending requests appear on approver's homepage
   - Listed under "For You Today" section
   - Email notification received

2. **Review Details:**
   - Approver can view:
     - Employee name and request details
     - Description provided by employee
     - Total vacation days balance
     - Days already used
     - Impact if approved

3. **Approval Decision:**
   - Click to approve or deny request
   - Add reason for denial (if denying)
   - Submit approval

4. **Response:**
   - Employee receives email notification of decision
   - Status updated in their Time Off tab
   - Approved leave appears in calendar

### 4.3 Policy Configuration for Approval

**Approver Assignment:**
- Set specific approvers for each time off policy
- Assign workers to policies
- Multiple approval levels supported

**Request Rules:**
- Set minimum/maximum leave length restrictions
- Enable/disable retrospective leave requests
- Allow workers to modify approved time off
- Set waiting periods for new hires
- Enable hourly or daily leave requests

**Notification Settings:**
- Automatic notifications to approvers
- Email confirmations to employees
- Status update notifications

### 4.4 Slack Integration

**PTO by Deel in Slack:**
- Request PTO directly from Slack
- Managers approve/deny from Slack
- Track and analyze PTO usage from Slack
- Speed up approvals for time off, raises, and PTO payouts

**Accounting Integration:**

### 5.5 Payroll Reporting

## 7. US-Specific Considerations

### 7.1 Multi-State Compliance Complexity

**Challenge:**
- Different requirements across 50 states plus D.C.
- 18 states with mandatory paid sick leave
- Varying accrual rates and carryover rules
- Different payout requirements at termination

**Deel Solution:**
- State-by-state compliance guides
- Pre-configured policy templates per state
- Automatic compliance checking
- PEO services for full compliance management
- Expert consultation available

### 7.2 Unlimited vs. Limited PTO

**Unlimited PTO Option:**
- Deel supports unlimited allowance policies
- No set accrual limit
- Employees can request as much as needed
- Viable in states with no federal mandate
- Requires careful policy definition and manager training

**Limited PTO Option:**
- Traditional accrual-based policies
- Specific number of days per year
- Accrual calculation methods
- Carryover and expiry policies
- More predictable payout liabilities

### 7.3 No Federal Sick Leave Mandate

**Strategic Implication:**
- US employers have more flexibility than international counterparts
- Deel supports this flexibility with customizable sick leave policies
- Critical for multi-state employers to stay compliant
- Deel PEO helps manage state-specific requirements
